Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about SMM Panel Easy.

What is SMM Panel Easy?

SMM Panel Easy is a social media marketing panel that provides services like followers, likes, views, and more for all major platforms. We offer the cheapest prices with instant delivery and 24/7 support.

Is it safe to use?

Yes, all our services are 100% safe. We use secure methods that comply with platform guidelines. Your account safety is our top priority.

How fast is the delivery?

Most orders start within seconds. Delivery time depends on the service and quantity. We aim for the fastest delivery possible while maintaining quality.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available for services not delivered as promised. Each service has specific refund policies. Contact support if you have any issues with your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Stripe, cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and many more regional payment methods worldwide.

Is payment secure?

Yes, all transactions are protected with SSL encryption. We never store your payment details. All payments are processed through trusted payment gateways.

Is there a minimum deposit?

The minimum deposit amount varies by payment method. Most methods have a low minimum of $1–$5 to get started easily.

How do I place an order?

Sign up, add funds to your account, navigate to New Order, select a service, enter your link and quantity, then confirm. Your order will start automatically.

Can I cancel an order?

Orders can be cancelled if they haven't started yet. Once processing begins, cancellations depend on the service type. Contact support for assistance.

How do I track my order?

After placing an order, go to your Orders page to see real-time status updates. You'll see the current count and remaining quantity for each order.

How do I become a reseller?

Simply sign up and you can start reselling immediately. Use our API to connect your own website or create a child panel with your own branding.

What is a child panel?

A child panel is your own branded SMM panel powered by our infrastructure. You can set your own prices, add your logo, and manage your own customers.

Are there API docs available?

Yes, full API documentation is available in your account dashboard. Our API is compatible with all major SMM panel scripts and supports JSON responses.
