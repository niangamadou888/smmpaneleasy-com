Telegram Growth FAQs

Common questions about growing your Telegram channel with our services.

Why does my Telegram channel need more members?

When people find a channel with only 100 members, they usually skip it. Channels with thousands of members look trustworthy and established. That social proof makes new visitors actually join and stick around.

How fast will I see results?

Most orders start within minutes. Members and views begin appearing quickly. We deliver gradually to keep growth looking natural and safe for your channel.

Do you need admin access to my channel?

Never. We only need your public channel link. We never ask for admin rights, passwords, or any sensitive access. Your channel stays completely under your control.

Is this safe for my channel?

Yes. Our methods are completely safe. We've helped thousands of channels grow without any issues. Real accounts, gradual delivery, and natural-looking growth protect your channel.

What is the cheapest Telegram service you offer?

Post views are our most affordable service, starting from just $0.02 per 1,000 views. They're great for boosting the appearance of your channel's activity and making posts look popular immediately.

Can I buy Telegram reactions?

Yes. We offer all emoji reactions available on Telegram, including 👍 ❤️ 🔥 and more. Reactions start from $0.10/1K and begin delivering within minutes of placing your order.

What are Telegram channel boosts?

Channel boosts are a Telegram Premium feature that unlocks custom reactions, stories, and other perks for your channel. We offer genuine boosts from premium accounts, starting from $2.00 per boost.

Do you offer auto-views for new posts?

Yes. Our auto-views service automatically sends views to every new post you publish for a set period. Set it once and all future posts get boosted — no manual ordering needed.

Does my channel need to be public?

Yes. Your Telegram channel must be public (with a public username or invite link) before placing an order. Once delivery is complete, you can adjust your channel settings as needed.

Can I buy members for a private Telegram group?

For private groups we require a valid invite link. Channel members work only with public channels or public invite links. Contact our support team for options tailored to private groups.

Will members leave after I purchase them?

Some minor drops are normal over time. Most of our member packages come with a refill guarantee — if the count drops within the warranty period, we top it back up at no extra cost.

How do I share my channel link for an order?

Copy your channel's public username (e.g., t.me/yourchannel) or the invite link. Paste it into the order form when checking out. No other information is needed from you.

How do I get started as a Telegram SMM reseller?

Register a free account, add funds, and request API access from your dashboard. You'll receive your API key along with full documentation to start integrating and reselling within minutes.

Can I create my own branded Telegram SMM panel?

Yes. Our child panel system lets you launch a fully white-labeled platform under your own domain and brand. Your clients never know we power the backend. Setup is quick and free.

Do resellers get lower prices?

Yes. Reseller accounts get access to lower wholesale rates than regular users. Volume-based tiers unlock even deeper discounts as your monthly spend grows. Contact us to discuss custom pricing.
